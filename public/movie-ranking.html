Sam's Favorite Movies of All Time

An ever changing top 4 list of Sam's favorite movies

Moonlight

This is honestly one of the most beautiful movies of all time. It's very personal to me and it always an emotional experience to watch

Homecoming

The greatest concert film of all time, buy the greatest performer of all time. The Queen Mother of the House of Renaissance, Beyonce Giselle Knowles-Carter

Paddington 2

My feel good movie. Whenever I feel down, this movie never fails to make me feel better. But I watch it sparingly because I don't want it to lose it's magic.

Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse

An animated masterpiece. Spiderman has always been my favorite superhero because I feel like I am him lol. It's also a great story about the responsibility of being a hero.
